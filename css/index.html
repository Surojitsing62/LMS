About

The Calcutta Technical School is a very old heritage of the city Kolkata. It is establish on 1927. The library of the CTS is maintained by Mr. Surojit Sing & Mr. Ayan Sen. Students can view, orders , lends books from the library. For this he/she should open a library account and then he get the all services of the CTS Library. But the number of maximum provided books among the students vary to their current standard. Such as student of 1st Year can get 2 maximumn books, 2nd year students can get 3 maximum books, 3rd year students can get 4 maximum books
